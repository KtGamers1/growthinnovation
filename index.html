Privacy Policy

Welcome to Growth Innovation. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect essential business information such as Name, City, Address, Phone, and Email to list you on our public directory. For authentication, we collect your Email address securely via Firebase Authentication.

2. Public Display & Toggles

By default, business contact details are public. However, we provide "Show to Public" toggles for your Phone and Email during registration. If unchecked, this data remains hidden from public view and is only accessible by the platform administrators.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for database management and secure authentication. We do not sell your data to any third-party marketing agencies.

Terms & Conditions

By using Growth Innovation, you agree to the following terms:

1. Platform Nature & Liability

Growth Innovation is an open-source, free directory. We manually approve listings to prevent spam, but we are strictly NOT responsible for the quality of products, services, or any fraudulent activities conducted by businesses listed on our platform. Users must exercise their own discretion.

2. Peer Review Policy

To maintain platform integrity, all business registrations and user reviews go through a 24-Hour Peer Review process by our administrative team. We reserve the right to reject or delete any listing or review that contains hate speech, fake information, or violates community guidelines.

3. Monetization & Refunds

We offer optional "Boost" features to increase listing visibility. Payments made for Boost plans are final. Refunds are strictly NOT applicable under any circumstances once a plan is activated.
