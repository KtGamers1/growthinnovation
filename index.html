Are you responsible for business quality?

No. We are a free and open source directory. We verify listings for spam, but we are not responsible for any bad experiences, quality of service, or transactions you make with any listed business.

No. Once a post is boosted using our monetization plans, the Refund is strictly NOT applicable. Please read the T&C before proceeding.

How does the Review System work?
