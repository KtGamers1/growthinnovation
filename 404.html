Platform Tutorial

How to Set Up Your Business

1 Create an Account

First, you need an account. Click the Sign In button on the top right, switch to 'Register', and create your account securely. .

2 Go to Business Portal

Once logged in, click on "Portal" at the top right. This is your personal dashboard. .

3 Register Your Business

Click on the "+ Register New" button. Fill in all details carefully. Upload good images (max 10), set your category, and optionally add your social media links like Instagram/WhatsApp. Don't forget to list your Menu/Items with prices!

4 Add Offers / Posts

Once your business is approved by Admin, you will see an "Add Offer/Post" button in your Portal. Click it to create timed offers (e.g., 20% off today) that will appear directly on your business profile!

Apna Business Kaise Setup Karein

1 Account Banayein

Sabse pehle, upar right side pe Sign In dabayein, 'Register' par click karein aur apna account banayein. .

2 Business Portal Par Jayein

Login karne ke baad, upar "Portal" par click karein. Ye aapka dashboard hai. .

3 Business Register Karein

Portal mein "+ Register New" dabayein. Apni details sahi se bharein. Achhi photos daalein, apni category select karein aur Instagram wagera ke link daalein. Apna Menu/Catalogue daalna mat bhoolna!

4 Offers / Post Daalein

Jab Admin aapka business Approve kar dega, tab aapko Portal mein "Add Offer/Post" ka button dikhega. Usse aap naye offers (jaise 20% discount) daal sakte hain jo seedha aapki profile par dikhenge!

Privacy Policy

Welcome to Growth Innovation. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect essential business information such as Name, City, Address, Phone, and Email to list you on our public directory. For authentication, we collect your Email address securely via Firebase Authentication.

2. Public Display & Toggles

By default, business contact details are public. However, we provide "Show to Public" toggles for your Phone and Email during registration. If unchecked, this data remains hidden from public view and is only accessible by the platform administrators.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for database management and secure authentication. We do not sell your data to any third-party marketing agencies.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can edit your business details or permanently delete your listing and associated images at any time through your Business Portal.
